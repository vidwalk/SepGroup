Yoga for men

This class started in 2010 and has been held every term time Thursday evening since then. It comprises all of the usual elements of traditional Hatha Yoga including: centring via breath awareness, postures/stretching (asanas), breathing practices (pranayama), sound practices (mantra) and guided relaxation (often in the form of Yoga Nidra). Some of the class will be in a flowing, dynamic style.		 10DKK

Be part of the combined energy of up to 500 yogis practising together and reap the mental and physically rewards of this 1 hour outdoor class. SEXY body
